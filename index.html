Digestive Tycoon

Your Mission

In Singapore, students eat heavy meals from Laksa to Kaya Toast. Your job is to manage the Chemical Digestion process. If you fail to break down nutrients, they ferment in the gut, causing System Stress (bloating). Survive as many days as possible without a "System Failure" (100% Stress)!

How to Play

1. The Biological Match

  • 🍞 Amylase: Targets Carbohydrates (Starches/Sugars).
  • 🍗 Pepsin: Targets Proteins (Meat/Eggs).
  • 🥑 Lipase: Targets Fats (Oils/Coconut Milk).

2. Manage Resources

  • ⚡ ATP Energy: Every enzyme click costs 10 ATP. Don't run out!
  • 🤢 System Stress: Undigested food becomes waste/gas. High stress reduces ATP recovery.
  • 🌙 Day Cycle: 5 meals total. The "Supper" is the hardest to digest!
